About

Etwan is a small but cool app that was made for users to find out the hidden areas of Google that sadly no one searches - the 2nd page. Now, you porbably won't get results as good as you will in the 1st page but in some situations like if you are stuck on a question with limited ammount of answers on the internet, the 2nd page is super useful. I hope this app helps someone out!
